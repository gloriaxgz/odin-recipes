Whey Protein Shake

Keeping it simple

The important thing here is that a whey protein shake can provide you a quick and easy source of proteins, and that is
essential when you are trying to gain muscle mass. So consider having this supplement in your daily life.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Whey Protein Powder
  • 1 glass Juice or Milk (about 8 oz)

Instructions

  1. For a quick and easy protein shake, add 1/4 cup Whey Protein Concentrate to a glass of juice or milk. Stir and enjoy.
